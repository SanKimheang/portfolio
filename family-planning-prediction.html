Family Planning Unmet Need Predictor

An unmet need for family planning occurs when a woman is fecund and sexually active, but is not using any method of contraception, despite wanting to space her next child (spacing unmet need) or stop childbearing altogether (limiting unmet need).

This interactive dashboard implements four predictive models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, LightGBM, and XGBoost) to calculate the likelihood that a woman faces an unmet family planning need. Adjust the demographic parameters in the form below to run real-time inference and view key individual risk factors.
